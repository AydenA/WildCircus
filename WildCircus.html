About Us

  • Family

    Animals are not our properties, they are part of our family, they don't obey us, they work with us to satisfy you. And we care for all of them as much as we care for our brothers and sisters.

  • Experience

    This year, it will be our 100th anniversary. 100 years of constant work to satisfy you more and more, to listen to your every wish.

  • Accessibility

    In order to welcome every person who wants to join, our entire circus is accessible to everyone, with any disability.

  • Innovation

    Every year, our entire show changes. The members do not leave but create new things. Do not hesitate to discover what we prepared for you this year.
